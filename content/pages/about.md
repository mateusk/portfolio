My background is in Interactive Media, UX Design, Software Development, Prototyping, and Media Arts. I have more than 10 years of experience working in different countries in multidisciplinary teams, in the cultural, entertainment and automotive industry. I am skilled in User Experience (UX) design and methods, modern web development technology stack (JavaScript ES6, TypeScript, Node.js, React.js, Vue.js), C++/OpenFrameworks, Python, TouchDesigner, and other prototyping tools/frameworks. I hold a Master of Arts - MA focused in Media Arts/Interdisciplinary Poetics from Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro. As an artist, for more than 10 years I have participated of many festivals, residencies and exhibitions, including a solo show in Rio de Janeiro in 2016.
